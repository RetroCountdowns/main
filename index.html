Creating Countdowns on Any Device

You don’t need a Windows computer to make countdowns. Our tool works smoothly on Android, tablets, or any modern device with a browser. It’s fast, responsive, and easy to use — anytime, anywhere.

FAQ

Is an account required to create a countdown?

Yes, you need to be logged in to create and manage your countdowns.

Can I edit my countdown after creating it?

Yes, you can edit your countdown any time by logging into your account and selecting it from your list.

Can I change the language of the countdown page?

Yes, the countdown page is multilingual and will automatically switch based on your browser’s settings.

Are any personal data stored when creating an account?

No, we do not store any personal data. Authentication is handled securely via Firebase, and no additional data is saved.

How can I share my countdown so that people around the world can see it?

After creating your countdown, you’ll automatically receive a unique link. You can share this link with anyone – they can open it in any browser and view your countdown.
